#PromptFirst

Prompt
smarter.
Use less.

Most people generate first and think second. A typical session: type something vague, see it's wrong, try again… 15 times. One precise prompt changes everything.

1
What is the subject? Be specific — not "a cat" but "a tabby cat sleeping on a red armchair"
2
What style or mood? Photorealistic, cartoon, vintage, dark, warm — name it exactly
3
What is the context? Indoors, sunset, city street — give the AI a full picture
4
What should NOT be in it? Blurry background? Extra people? Text? Block it before it appears.
5
What format do you need? Square? Landscape? 4K? Portrait? Specify it now, not after 10 tries.
