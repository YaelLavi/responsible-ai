Best Practice 1
The "Think Twice" filter
Ask yourself: do I actually need this image, or is it just digital slop?
Best Practice 2
Draft before
you craft
Ask a text AI to write the perfect image prompt first. Text costs 10× less than images.
Best Practice 3
Use negative
prompts
Tell the AI what NOT to include — blurry hands, extra text, wrong colors. Get it right in v1.
