# Social Media Campaign: The Invisible Cost of AI
**Objective:** Raise awareness about the environmental impact of Generative AI and promote "Intentional Creation."
**Target Audience:** General users, AI hobbyists, and digital creators.

---

## Post 1: The "Digital Sip" 💧
**Headline: Every AI Prompt is a Sip of Water.**

**The Hook:** Did you know that asking an AI just 25–50 questions "consumes" about **500ml of water**? That’s a full plastic bottle of fresh water used just to cool the servers that process your thoughts.

**The Context:**
* **AI vs. Search:** A generative AI query uses **10x more energy** than a traditional Google Search.
* **The "Human" Tax:** Saying "Hi," "Thanks," or "How are you?" to an AI might feel polite, but it’s a wasted compute cycle that adds up to millions of liters of water globally.

**Best Practices for a "Dry" AI Habit:**
1.  **Stack Your Thoughts:** Don’t send five messages. Gather your context and ask one comprehensive question. 
2.  **Skip the Pleasantries:** The AI doesn't have feelings, but the planet has a limit. Skip the "Hi/Thanks" in separate prompts.
3.  **The Search First Rule:** If you just need a link, a weather update, or a simple fact, use a **standard search engine**. It’s non-generative and uses a fraction of the power.

---

## Post 2: The "Funny Image" Budget 🚗
**Headline: Is that "Cyberpunk Cat" worth a 4-mile drive?**

**The Hook:** Generating one high-res AI image uses as much energy as **charging your smartphone halfway**. If you run 5 iterations to get the "perfect" funny meme, you’ve just used the energy equivalent of **driving a gasoline car for 4 miles.**

**The Math:**
* **1 Image** ≈ 6 minutes of a microwave at full power.
* **5 Iterations** ≈ A small petrol car's emissions for a quick trip to the grocery store.

**Best Practices for Intentional Creation:**
1.  **The "Think Twice" Filter:** Do you really need this image, or is it just "digital slop"?
2.  **Draft Before You Craft:** Before using a heavy Image Model, ask a text-based AI (which is much "cheaper") to **write the perfect prompt for you** first. 
3.  **Use Negative Prompts:** Instead of re-generating 10 times to remove a "blurry hand," use a negative prompt to get it right in version one.

---

## Post 3: The "Silly Video" Reality Check 🍿
**Headline: A 5-second "Fun" Video = 1 Hour of Microwaving.**

**The Hook:** AI video is the "Heavyweight Champion" of carbon emissions. Generating a short, 5-second AI video clip consumes the same electricity as **running your microwave for a full hour.**

**The Comparison:**
* **Video Intensity:** The energy required to generate video quadruples every time you double the length. 
* **The Cost:** 1 minute of AI video can generate more CO2 than **boiling 20 kettles of water.**

**Best Practices for Video Builders:**
1.  **Storyboard First:** Never "guess" with video. Map out your scene on paper or in a text doc before hitting 'Generate'.
2.  **Low-Res First:** Use low-resolution "previews" to check motion before committing to a high-def, energy-intensive final render.
3.  **The "Value" Test:** Use video for things that *can't* be filmed (like space travel). For a "silly meme," a static image or a GIF is 100x more sustainable.

---

> **Pro-Tip:** When building your website or podcast, use these stats as "Did You Know?" callouts to keep users engaged with the data.
