AI is "free." Except it's not. Every prompt you send. Every image you generate. Every chatbot conversation. It costs energy. It costs water. It costs the planet. Most people don't know this — and that's exactly why I'm here. I'm not telling you to stop using AI. I believe AI can do real good in the right hands. But free is a word we need to rethink. The data centers running these models consume enormous amounts of electricity and water to stay cool. The exact numbers change as the technology evolves — but whether it's 1 liter or 0.75ml per session, the direction is the same: we spend natural resources every time we click "generate." And natural resources are not expandable. I care about this because I want my grandchildren to grow up with green gardens and clean air. That future doesn't happen by accident — it starts with awareness. So let's talk about it. Honestly. Out loud. Use AI. Use it wisely. — What's one thing you didn't know about AI's environmental cost? Drop it in the comments. #ResponsibleAI #AIAwareness #Sustainability #DigitalMindfulness
