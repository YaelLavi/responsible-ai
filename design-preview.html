Responsible AI Awareness Campaign

Every AI request costs something. Use it wisely.

AI isn't free. Every prompt spends energy. Every image uses water. This campaign makes the invisible cost visible — and gives you simple habits to do better.

Take Action Learn More
Environmental Impact

The cost you don't see

1 AI Image
= Fully charging your smartphone
~2.91 Wh of electricity per generation
5-second AI video
= Running your microwave for 1+ hour
~944 Wh — 275× more than a text prompt

"A well-written prompt can cut image iterations from 20 down to 2–3. That's 80–90% less energy — with better results."

Prompt-First Principle
6 Habits to Adopt

Small shifts. Real impact.

  • Prompt before you generate.
    2 minutes writing beats 20 minutes regenerating bad outputs.
  • Batch your requests.
    Combine related questions into one clear, structured request.
  • Use search for simple lookups.
    A regular search uses ~10× less energy than an AI query.
  • Know when good enough is enough.
    The 15th version is rarely better than the 3rd.
  • Treat AI video as a premium.
    Use it purposefully, not casually.
  • Regenerate with a reason.
    Name what was wrong. Fix the prompt. Don't regenerate blind.
