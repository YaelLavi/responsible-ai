Take Action

Small habits. Visible impact.

You don't have to stop using AI. You just have to use it with your eyes open. Here's where to start.

Six Commitments

Your personal responsible AI checklist.

0 of 6 habits committed to

Prompt before I generate

I will spend 60 seconds planning before I generate, so my first attempt is my best attempt.

Batch my requests

I will group related AI tasks into single sessions instead of returning repeatedly throughout the day.

Regenerate with a reason

I will only regenerate when I can name what I'm changing — not just hoping for something different.

Choose the right tool

I will ask whether a search, a template, or my own judgment could handle this before defaulting to AI.

Know when good enough is enough

I will stop iterating when the result serves its purpose, not when it's perfect.

Treat AI video as premium

I will use AI video generation intentionally, for work where it genuinely adds value.
